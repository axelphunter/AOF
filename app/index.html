The Art of Floatation

One of the most advanced forms of relaxation and recovery to date; zero-gravity time in a specially designed pod, housing a scientifically designed water environment to suspend and relieve the body from the stresses of gravity. Sensory deprivation, much like wearing a blindfold, can heighten some senses such as touch while depriving all other senses of light, sound, heat and gravity. This leaves the mind to work at its most efficient, allowing physical and mental recovery, extreme relaxation, accelerated learning, goal visualising and general well-being as a result of achieving a meditative like state during floatation.
