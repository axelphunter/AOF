The Art of Floatation

The most advanced form of relaxation/recovery to date. Zero gravity time in a specially designed pod, housing a scientifically designed water environment to suspend the body and relieve from the stresses of gravity. Sensory deprivation, much like wearing a blindfold can heighten other sense such as touch, depriving all senses, such as light, sound, thermoception and even gravity, can leave the mind to work at it's most efficient. Dealing with physical and mental recovery, extreme relaxation, accelerated learning, goal visualising and general well-being as a result of achieving a meditative like state during floatation.
